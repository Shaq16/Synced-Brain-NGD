# Artificial Intelligence (AI)

## What is Artificial Intelligence?
Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and make decisions.

## Key Concepts

### 1. Machine Learning (ML)
Machine Learning is a subset of AI where systems learn from data instead of being explicitly programmed.

### 2. Deep Learning
Deep Learning uses neural networks with multiple layers to model complex patterns in data.

### 3. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
NLP enables machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language.

### 4. Computer Vision
Computer Vision allows machines to interpret and analyze visual data like images and videos.

---

## Applications of AI

- Chatbots and virtual assistants
- Recommendation systems (Netflix, Amazon)
- Self-driving cars
- Medical diagnosis
- Fraud detection

---

## Advantages of AI

- Automates repetitive tasks
- Improves efficiency and accuracy
- Handles large volumes of data
- Enables intelligent decision-making

---

## Limitations of AI

- Requires large amounts of data
- High computational cost
- Can be biased if training data is biased
- Lack of human-like understanding

---

## AI in Modern Systems

AI is widely used in modern systems such as:
- Search engines
- Voice assistants (Siri, Alexa)
- Autonomous systems
- Smart recommendation engines

---

## AI and Vector Databases

AI systems often use vector databases to store embeddings of data for semantic search and retrieval. This is especially useful in Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems.

---

## Summary

Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries by enabling machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. It plays a crucial role in modern applications and continues to evolve rapidly.
